It hosts user-submitted videos, which can range in duration from 3 seconds to 10 minutes.
TikTok is a popular social media app that allows users to create, watch, and share 15-second videos shot on mobile devices or webcams.
With its personalized feeds of quirky short videos set to music and sound effects, the app is notable for its addictive quality and high levels of engagement.

Target users

TikTok is most popular with Younger Generations:
  • Ages 10-19 are 25% of users.
  • Ages 20-29 are 22.4% of users.
  • Ages 30-39 is 21.7% of users.
  • Ages 40-49 is 20.3% of users.

Trend

A variety of trends have risen within TikTok, including memes, lip-synced songs, and comedy videos.
Duets, a feature that allows users to add their own video to an existing video with the original content's audio, have sparked many of these trends.
