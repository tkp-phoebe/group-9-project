NEW MEDIA





New Media and Social Media

Different from conventional media like magazines and radio, New media requires digital technology.
It is no longer a one-way communication Instead, people can participate in the conversation and interact with others.


Examples of new media and social media

  1. Websites
  2. Social media platforms
    3. E.g. Instagram, Discord, Snapchat
  3. Video sharing platforms
    4. E.g. YouTube, Vimeo, Twitch

Reasons it is important

It has greatly enhanced the connection between people all over the world.
Providing an exit for people to express thoughts and opinions.
It brings efficiency in the distribution of information globally.
Important news and information can be received in real-time. A cost-effective tool in marketing.

Influence to the world

It changes our living pattern: business mode, access to information.
New media and social media establish the connection between certain parties: Businesses and consumers, organisations and audiences, political officers and electorate.
