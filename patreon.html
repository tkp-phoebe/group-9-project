Background

Patreon is a platform launched on May 2, 2013.
It established by a company based in San Francisco, by Sam Yan and Jack Conte.
It contains 3 million monthly active patrons until now.

Target users

Creators are users who upload contents to the platform regularly, mainly
  • YouTubers,
  • artists,
  • writers,
  • podcasters.
Patrons are users who join the membership and support creators’ works in
  • videos,
  • podcasts,
  • comedy,
  • comics,
  • games
  • and education.

How it runs?

It allows creators to run subscription services , in order to earn a monthly income.
Creators provide different benefits (art works) to patrons according to their membership levels.
Patrons with subscription can go through the past works for free.
Patrons will be charged monthly according to the amount of works uploaded by creators.

Advantages

  1. Allowing creators and subscribers to interact more
  2. Creators can rely on the relatively stable income to support their creations
  3. A platform that protects personal privacy
  4. High degree of freedom that any form of arts are encouraged

Concerns

  1. Patreon will not promote your page
  2. Heavier charge as the number of subscribers increase
  3. Patreon have the right to delete your page when received complaints
